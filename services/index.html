Four dimensions reviewed

Accuracy
Does the documentation reflect the current state of the product? Where is it drifting?
Structure
Is the information architecture logical? Can a first-time reader find what they need without asking someone?
Ownership
Does every section have a named owner? Is there a clear process for keeping it current?
Findability
Are pages connected? Does every page link to the next logical step? Can search surface the right content?

Common questions

Questions founders ask before booking.

What access do you need to my documentation?

Read-only access is all I need. If your documentation is public-facing, I do not need any access at all. If it lives behind a login, a guest or viewer account is enough. I do not need editor access at any point during the audit.

What if my documentation is too small or too large?

The audit works for any size. For very small documentation sets, the review is faster and the report more focused. For larger sets, I prioritise the most critical sections and flag where a deeper engagement would be valuable. The price is the same either way.

What if we are not ready? Our docs are a mess.

That is exactly the right time for an audit. The audit is a diagnostic tool, not a quality check. I am not here to judge your documentation. I am here to map what is broken and tell you how to fix it. The messier the starting point, the more valuable the output.

What happens after the audit?

You receive the report and the action plan. Some teams implement the recommendations themselves. Others come back to engage me for implementation. There is no pressure either way. The audit stands on its own as a complete deliverable.

How is this different from hiring a technical writer?

Hiring a technical writer fixes individual pages. The audit fixes the system those pages live in. If the system is broken, new pages written by anyone will drift and fail at the same rate as the old ones. The audit identifies the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Can I see examples of your work before booking?

Yes. Every piece of work I have produced is live and publicly accessible. The proof section at the top of this page links to five of them directly. Read them. If the quality, structure, and thinking on display match what you need, we are a good fit.
